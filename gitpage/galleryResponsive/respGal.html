Foxes belong to the dog family and use similar facial expressions and body postures to pet dogs, such as wagging their tails when greeting family members
An adult male’s roar can be heard up to 8km away. A female lion needs 5kg of meat a day. A male needs 7kg or more a day.
Cats use their whiskers to detect if they can fit through a space.
These amazing apes generally have long lives – in captivity they can live for 50-60 years, and in the wild, 30-40 years.
Tigers have night vision that is six times better than that of humans, which helps them hunt successfully in the dark.
Cows are social animals, and they naturally form large herds. And like people, they will make friends and bond to some herd members, while avoiding others
