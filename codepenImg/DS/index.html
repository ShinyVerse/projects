Don't Starve Tribute

From the makers Klei, Don't Starve offers gamers a unique gameplay experience of survival in a seeminlgy cutesy world. The more you play the more you realise this game is unforgiving. Appearing sandbox, but with an ability to drive you batty the game challenges you not only to keep your character fed, but also sane and safe from harsh elements, deadly monsters and your own mind's tricks.

Characters:

Don't Starve, Don't Starve Together and Don't Starve: Shipwrecked have an assortment of characters available to the player. Each comes with their own strengths and weaknesses. So choose wisely! Once you choose you cannot change your mind. Take a gander at the characters by clicking on them and see which entices you!
